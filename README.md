## Files

**File:** `Fig3-bdg.R`  
**Description:**  
*Determination of cluster marker genes and generation of plots featured in Figure 3, Panels B, D, and G.*

---

**File:** `Fig4.R`  
**Description:**  
*Up- and Downregulated genes from pseudobulk genotype comparisons shown in Figure 4.*

**Description:**  
*Differential expression analysis for bulk RNA-Seq of of control, IFNα, IFNβ, or IFNγ treated iMGs.  Relates to Figure 7.*

---

**File:** `Fig7-tu.R`  
**Description:**  
*Addition of bulk IFNα, IFNβ, and IFNγ module scores for scRNA-Seq data and creation of Figure 7, Panels T and U.*
